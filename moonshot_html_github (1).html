The Challenge

The Problem Space

Brilliant technical founders — PhDs, professors, researchers — building breakthrough technologies in quantum computing, carbon capture, satellite systems, cancer therapeutics, and AI.

But they're scientists, not business operators. They don't know their market, can't articulate value propositions to investors, and struggle to identify the right partnerships to scale.

My Role

As a Business Innovation Lead at CDL, I built a systematic approach to de-risk these moonshots:

  • Reviewed 200+ technical applications across 5 deep tech verticals
  • Conducted hundreds of founder interviews to assess technical novelty and market potential
  • Worked closely with 50-80 startups through 9-month intensive programs
  • Launched CDL Estonia focused on cybersecurity technologies

The Methodology

A systematic, repeatable framework for translating deep tech into investable ventures

STEP 1

Technical Diligence

Recruit expert scientists to validate technological novelty and feasibility

STEP 2

Market Validation

Help technical founders understand TAM/SAM/SOM and identify viable market opportunities

STEP 3

Narrative Shaping

Craft compelling investor memos and value propositions that bridge tech and business

STEP 4

Partnership Facilitation

Connect founders to world-class mentors, customers, and investors

STEP 5

Go-to-Market Strategy

Test business models, pricing strategies, and market entry sequences
